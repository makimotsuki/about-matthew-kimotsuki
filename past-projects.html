National Parks Website

In this project, I made a website showcasing some of my favorite national parks using HTML and CSS. I very much enjoy hiking and the outdoors, and wanted to be able to share valuable information to others who might be interested in visiting these national parks. I included interactive flip cards, weather trends, the best time to go, and best hikes to go on, organizing it in an inuitive interface. I was able to strengthen my understanding of front-end web development while also working on something that I am very passionate about.
